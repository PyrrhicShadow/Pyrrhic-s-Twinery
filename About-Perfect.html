Perfect

Perfect is an interactive poem written in Twine 2's Harlowe story format. This project was my "hello world" for Twine, but since then, it's become a freeform expression of the trappings of beauty and familiarity.

As a short poem, this project can be considered complete, though I do plan to add more to it from time to time, whenever inspiration strikes.
