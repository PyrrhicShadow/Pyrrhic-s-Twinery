Not Weird. Queer

Not Weird. Queer is a loosely autobiographical text-based video game about a trans teen going through middle school.

Currently, Not Weird. Queer is still in the alpha-af (pre-alpha) stage of developement.

Version 0.0.007

Version 0.0.007 includes a brand-new day 1 that acts as a sort of prologue. The new day 1 is still largely incomplete.

  • Known issue: when choosing a name, the Harlowe engine will occasionally display an error message. If you click "OK" on the message, the game will resume normally.
  • Contains a new day 1 that serves as an introduction to the player character's psyche and world, providing context on their mental state. This new day 1 is heavily comprised of dummy text, as is much of part 2.
  • At the end of the game, Harlowe will export a summary of the player's choices to the developer.
    • The system for exporting the passage history is buggy and detects an infinite loop when there isn't one. I am investigating a workaround.
    • Learn more about what is collected and how it is used here.
  • v. 0.0.006 bug fixes:
    • Fixed the bug where the player is awarded with double points/penalty when they get bullied in history class on part 2 day 9 (absolute day).
  • Known bug: if you input a name that is already the ally's name or your deadname, the Harlowe engine will run into an error.
